Manufacturing Telemetry Analytics & Dashboard Design (Deloitte Simulation)
OVERVIEW

This project was completed as part of the Deloitte Australia Data Analytics Job Simulation. The goal was to transform raw manufacturing telemetry data into actionable business insights using data modeling and advanced visualization techniques in Tableau.

BUSINESS UNDERSTANDING

    Problem: Identifying operational bottlenecks and high machine failure rates across global manufacturing plants.

    Stakeholders: Supply Chain Managers and Operations Directors seeking to optimize maintenance schedules and reduce production losses.

DATA UNDERSTANDING

    Source: Synthetic raw JSON telemetry data representing one month of activity from 4 global manufacturing plants.

    Key Metrics: Machine status messages, equipment downtime logs, and factory location data.

    Process: Handled data ingestion and structuring to prepare the raw status messages for analytical modeling.

MODELING AND EVALUATION

    Tools: Tableau & Excel.

    Methods: Developed custom calculated fields to quantify downtime accurately and designed a dynamic, cross-filtered executive dashboard.

    Success Metric: Successfully isolated the specific facilities and machine types responsible for the highest production losses.

RESULT AND RECOMMENDATION

    Action: Operations managers should prioritize targeted predictive maintenance for the specific machine types identified as high-risk in the dashboard.

    Impact: Implementing these data-driven maintenance schedules could significantly reduce future factory downtime and associated costs.
